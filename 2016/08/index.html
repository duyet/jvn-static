Từ Cựu sinh viên ICT

At JVN, I always feel like home. At JVN, I am aware that I am heard and I am cared. This home has given me precious treasures that I always embrace. It’s not only the knowledge that I’ve been taught but also the love that I’ve felt. Everyday at JVN, I see the passion on my friends’ faces and also their enthusiasm to learn and to share. Whenever my friends and I looked for an open discussion full of joy, care and share, we always had it. All new ideas are welcome here, even the most insane ones - I find this is the real pleasure of studying and working. One needs a vision for his life. And I’m grateful and proud to say that I’ve found and built mine at JVN, no doubt!

Lê Quang Thảo

Eiffel Scholarship Laureate ‘12
M.Eng. Télécom-ParisTech ’14
